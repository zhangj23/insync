Connect

Fostering meaningful connections and collaborative learning among students has become increasingly crucial. Students can have a difficult time transitioning to the college lifestyle. Through InSync, we aim to create a community that will enable students to find and connect with people who share similar academic and extracurricular interests.

Match

Discover new connections with our student matching feature! After filling out a brief interest form, we use your preferences, hobbies, and academic goals to pair you with like-minded peers. Whether you're looking for study partners, collaborative projects, or shared interests, our matching system helps you find the perfect fit for a more engaging and rewarding student experience.

Find Events

Never miss out on exciting opportunities with our event-finding feature! Tailored to your interests, this tool helps you discover upcoming campus events, workshops, and activities that match your preferences. Whether you're into academics, social events, or extracurriculars, easily find and join events that align with your passions and goals.
